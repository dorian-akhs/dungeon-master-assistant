# Silas Renn

## At a Glance

- **Role**: Brotherhood liaison to the eastern districts
- **Affiliation**: Brotherhood of the Iron Hand
- **Location**: Eastern districts, Vaucanheim
- **Disposition toward PCs**: Cautiously neutral — he's appeared at two Covenant meetings they attended, but hasn't interacted directly until now

## Appearance

Mid-forties. The kind of face that's easy to forget — deliberately so, you suspect. Average height, average build, unremarkable clothing. The only thing that distinguishes him is that he moves very quietly for someone his size.

## Personality

Pragmatic. Values information over ideology. Patient — the kind of person who has been playing a long game long enough that impatience has become physically uncomfortable. Not cruel, but capable of ruthlessness in service of goals he believes are correct.

## Background

Brotherhood liaison for at least seven years. Known to be effective in eastern district organizing and dispute resolution. No known history before Brotherhood involvement.

## Relationships

- **Brotherhood Eastern Command**: Officially reports to two senior figures; currently distrusts both

## History with the Party

### Session 1

Observed at a Brotherhood-attended Covenant coordination meeting. Oryn recognized him; they did not speak.

### Session 2

Present in the background during the party's covert entry to the Collapsed Tower. Did not intervene.

### Session 3

Approached [[Oryn]] and [[Petra]] in a Tanner's District alley after their sewer exit. Warned that the Collapsed Tower fallout would draw Brotherhood scrutiny, then pivoted to personal business: the Hollow Knight manufactory plans, if realized, violate the core premise of the eleven-year Brotherhood–Covenant treaty (no autonomous iron constructs in city limits at scale). Claimed mass-produced Hollow Knights would void the treaty and destabilize the city. Alleged eastern district leadership has been compromised by Covenant favors. Directed the party to meet him next at the [[Rusted Anchor]] in Dockside. [[Petra]]'s read: he believed what he said; not a performed lie.

## DM Secrets

Silas has a personal agenda: the Collapsed Tower once belonged to his family, confiscated by the Covenant during the city's expansion. His push against the Covenant isn't just institutional — it's a thirty-year grudge. He wants to be the one who brings them down.

His current Brotherhood superiors have been compromised. He knows this, has evidence, and has been sitting on it, waiting for leverage.

## Voice Notes

Speaks quietly and without inflection. Makes statements rather than questions. The effect is unsettling — it can feel like he already knows your answer. Doesn't use contractions when being serious.
