# Session 003: The Liaison in the Alley

> **Quick Summary**: Soaked and wounded after the tower escape, the party treated [[Oryn]]'s injury in a Tanner's District doorway — then [[Silas Renn]] found them. He laid out what the Hollow Knight schematics imply for the Brotherhood–Covenant treaty and admitted he can't trust his own chain of command. Afterward they scouted the [[Rusted Anchor]] (his chosen meet site), and [[Petra]]'s study of the plans revealed a worse truth: a key frequency granting one operator command over hundreds of units.

---

## Events

### Tanner's District — Alley Triage

The party emerged from the sewers after dark, wet and exposed. [[Oryn]]'s arm was cut by the iron golem; [[Petra]] used a healing kit successfully in a recessed doorway between tanneries.

### Same Alley — [[Silas Renn]]

[[Silas Renn]] approached deliberately. He referenced the mess in the Collapsed Tower and the Brotherhood's inevitable questions, but framed his visit as personal, not official business. He claimed the manufactory schematics change everything: if the Covenant builds what the plans describe, the eleven-year peace treaty is worthless and the city with it.

Over a long conversation he shared (as alleged fact from his perspective):
- Brotherhood–Covenant peace has held eleven years on terms limiting autonomous iron constructs in city limits
- The manufactory targets mass production of Hollow Knights — autonomous, no operator required
- He lacks proof beyond suspicion and his observation of the party's sewer exit
- Two superiors in eastern district command have received major Covenant "gifts" this year; he no longer knows which way they would fall

[[Petra]]'s Insight suggested [[Silas Renn]] believes his own story — not necessarily that every claim is objectively true, but he was not performing distress.

### Dockside District — [[Rusted Anchor]] (advance scout)

[[Silas Renn]] directed them to meet him the following night at the [[Rusted Anchor]]. The party scouted it first: a loud dockworkers' tavern where questions go unasked; a curtained back booth offers semi-private space.

### Safehouse — Schematics Study

That night, [[Petra]] studied the schematics closely (strong Arcana result). Hollow Knights are not merely autonomous — they are tuned to a key frequency allowing one holder to command all units simultaneously. At projected manufactory capacity, the scale approaches hundreds of constructs under a single operator.
