# Session 002: The Collapsed Tower

> **Quick Summary**: The party infiltrated the Collapsed Tower on behalf of a Brotherhood contact and discovered a manufactory with schematics for mass-produced autonomous constructs. [[Oryn]] was wounded by an iron golem guardian. They escaped through the sewers.

## Events

### Collapsed Tower — Infiltration

Party entered via a maintenance access point. Interior: four levels of deteriorating structure, partially repurposed as a workshop. Evidence of recent occupancy — fresh tools, new construction in the basement levels.

### Manufactory Level — Discovery

Bottom level contained a working manufactory. Partially assembled constructs visible. [[Petra]] secured a set of technical schematics. Iron golem guardian activated; combat ensued.

### Escape

[[Oryn]] took a significant wound from the golem's strike. Party retreated through a basement passage into the sewer network, emerging in the Tanner's District after dark.
