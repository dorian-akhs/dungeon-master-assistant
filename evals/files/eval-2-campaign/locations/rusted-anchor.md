# Rusted Anchor

## Overview

A working dockers' tavern in the Dockside District — loud, crowded, and practiced at not noticing what patrons prefer to keep private.

## Description

Noise, spilled beer, laborers decompressing after shifts. The back corner holds a booth partially screened by a curtain, usable for low-visibility meetings.

## Key Features

- Curtained rear booth for semi-private conversation
- Clientele that avoids prying

## Inhabitants

Dockworkers and associated trades; no named regulars established this session.

## History

[[Silas Renn]] named it as the follow-up meet site; the party scouted the tavern the night before that appointment.

## Connections

- **Dockside District**: Embedded in Vaucanheim's waterfront labor economy
