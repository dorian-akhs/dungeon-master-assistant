# Sister Elara

## At a Glance

- **Role**: Priest / sister of the Temple of the Veil; reliquary keeper
- **Affiliation**: Temple of the Veil
- **Location**: Temple of the Veil, Scholar's District, Tolvern
- **Disposition toward PCs**: Calm, assessing; cooperative because Thornwood sent them

---

## Appearance

Small stature, gray hair, calm eyes. Met the party by lamplight in the reliquary among locked cases and wrapped relics.

---

## Personality

Measured and deliberate. Speaks with weight when naming factions or threats. Does not rush a hard conversation.

---

## Background

Known to Thornwood as a trusted contact; supplied a plague sample the prior winter that changed his understanding of how the Ash Plague spreads.

---

## Relationships

- **Valdris Thornwood**: Trusted contact; warned him she might speak to his agents

---

## History with the Party

### Session 2

Received Cassiel and Lyra in the reliquary (Kael remained outside). Confirmed she saw a figure leave the alley behind Thornwood's shop two nights ago with an oilcloth bundle; could not identify the face. Identified a pin as Merchants' Guild but not the standard merchant chapter — named the Black Ledger as the sigil's association.
