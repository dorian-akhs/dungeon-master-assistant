# Valdris Thornwood

## At a Glance

- **Role**: Apothecary, researcher, reluctant conspirator
- **Affiliation**: Independent (formerly licensed under the Guild, license revoked)
- **Location**: Merchant's Quarter, Tolvern — small apothecary shop
- **Disposition toward PCs**: Cautious trust; he reached out to them, so he needs them, but he's not comfortable with it

## Appearance

Late fifties, lean with a slight stoop from years bent over workbenches. Long fingers perpetually stained with something orange-brown. Gray hair kept short. Watchful eyes that move to the window before he answers anything sensitive.

## Personality

Meticulous, careful, somewhat paranoid — but the paranoia is justified. Believes information is best kept compartmentalized. Cares deeply about the Ash Plague research and will accept risk for it that he wouldn't accept for anything else. Keeps people at arm's length until he decides to trust them.

## Background

Born and raised in Saltmere, a coastal town that was hit hard by the Ash Plague twenty years ago. Came to Tolvern to study under the Guild's licensed apothecaries, eventually worked his way to independent research. His Guild license was revoked eight months ago under circumstances he describes as "politically motivated."

His fifteen years of Ash Plague research includes a treatment method that doesn't depend on Veilstone — which makes it dangerous to the Guild's monopoly.

## Relationships

- **Unknown (Guild operative)**: Someone inside the Guild has been monitoring him for months

## History with the Party

### Session 1

The PCs were given Thornwood's name as a potential contact by an unnamed source. They met him briefly — he was cautious, gave them minimal information, agreed to meet again. Cassiel kept the contact letter he sent them.

## DM Secrets

Thornwood has been making two copies of every research document for the past six months — one he keeps, one he gave to Pell in the Sunken Archive. He hasn't told the PCs this. He's testing them before he reveals the backup exists.

His Guild license wasn't just "revoked" — he made a deal: silence in exchange for keeping his shop. He's been violating that deal by continuing research.

## Voice Notes

Speaks quietly and precisely. Rarely asks questions — states things and waits for confirmation. Uses technical vocabulary naturally but can translate when needed. Does not small-talk.
