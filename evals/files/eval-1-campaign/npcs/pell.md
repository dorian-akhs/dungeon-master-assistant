# Pell

## At a Glance

- **Role**: Archivist in the Sunken Archive
- **Affiliation**: Independent scholar community maintaining old records beneath Tolvern
- **Location**: Sunken Archive, undercity
- **Disposition toward PCs**: Businesslike; willing to trade information for a favor

---

## Appearance

Elderly gnome. Workspace crowded with scrolls; a desk that looks structurally dubious. Barely looked up when the party entered.

---

## Personality

Values records and leverage over coin. Frames exchanges as mutual necessity rather than charity.

---

## Background

Maintains archives in the sunken undercity district. Has been holding a copy of Thornwood's formula as insurance against Guild seizure.

---

## Relationships

- **Valdris Thornwood**: Holds a backup copy of Thornwood's formula at Thornwood's request

---

## History with the Party

### Session 2

Acknowledged Thornwood had sent the party. Offered the formula copy in exchange for retrieving materials from the Merchant's Hall Records Office — claimed his permitted access has been illegally blocked for three months. Alleged the records contain evidence the Black Ledger has operated in Tolvern for at least thirty years, predating the current Guild Master.
