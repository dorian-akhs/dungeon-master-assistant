# Campaign Timeline

## Session 1 — Campaign Start
- PCs recruited by unknown contact to meet apothecary Valdris Thornwood
- First meeting with Thornwood in Merchant's Quarter
- Thornwood confirmed he has information about the Ash Plague that the Guild wants suppressed
- PCs agreed to help Thornwood; received contact letter as proof of relationship
- Location discovered: Merchant's Quarter, Tolvern (general)
- NPC introduced: Valdris Thornwood

## Session 2 — The Notebook Trail
- Thornwood briefed the party on a storeroom break-in targeting his Veilstone-independent plague formula; party received a notebook of contacts
- Sister Elara (Temple of the Veil) reported a figure with a Black Ledger pin leaving Thornwood's alley with an oilcloth bundle
- Party descended to the Sunken Archive and met Pell the Archivist, who holds a backup copy of the formula and requested retrieval of records from the Merchant's Hall Records Office in exchange
- NPC introduced: Sister Elara
- NPC introduced: Pell (Archivist)
- Location discovered: Sunken Archive (undercity)
- Location discovered: Temple of the Veil (Scholar's District — visited)
