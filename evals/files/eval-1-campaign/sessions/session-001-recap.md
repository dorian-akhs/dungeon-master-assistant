# Session 001: First Contact

> **Quick Summary**: The party made contact with Valdris Thornwood, a disgraced apothecary conducting unsanctioned Ash Plague research. He revealed that someone in the Merchants' Guild is suppressing his work, and asked for their help. The party agreed, receiving a contact letter and instructions to return the next morning.

---

## Events

### Merchant's Quarter — Street Approach

The PCs approached Thornwood's shop in the early evening. Kael noticed they were being observed from a rooftop — brief, then gone. No confrontation.

### Thornwood's Apothecary — First Meeting

Thornwood received the party with obvious anxiety. He confirmed:
- He has developed a Veilstone-independent Ash Plague treatment
- The Guild has been pressuring him to abandon the research
- His Guild license was revoked eight months ago
- He believes someone within the Guild knows about the treatment and wants to control or destroy it

He did not reveal the location of his research notes. He asked the party to return the next morning, saying he needed to "confirm some things" overnight.
