# Campaign Timeline

## Session 1 — Arrival in Tolvern
- Party arrived in Tolvern; established lodgings at the Silver Flagon
- NPC introduced: Mira (Silver Flagon owner)
- Location discovered: Silver Flagon, Tanner's District
- Location discovered: Tanner's District (general)

## Session 2 — The Tip
- Party investigated the "Guild-adjacent" observer Mira had flagged
- Traced observer to a warehouse in the Dockside District — found evidence of Serpent Crown cargo manifests
- Location discovered: Dockside Warehouse District
- NPC introduced: Dross (fence, indirect contact via Mira)

## Session 3 — The Fence
- Party engaged Dross to evaluate cargo manifests
- Confirmed the Crown was moving contraband through legitimate merchant fronts
- Dross refused to identify any specific merchants — "not worth it"
- Evidence secured: copies of three falsified cargo manifests

## Session 4 — Recovery and Relationship
- Low-intensity session; party recovered from injuries sustained in Session 3 street encounter
- Cassiel investigated Temple district rumors about Crown infiltration
- Party grew closer as a group; significant roleplay between Morwen and Dax
- Cassiel learned: at least one Temple acolyte has been feeding information to the Crown

## Session 5 — The Missing Merchant
- Mira mentioned Aldous Krenn's disappearance (had been a regular customer for years)
- Party began investigating; found his shop locked and unattended for at least a week
- Spoke with a neighbor: Aldous had seemed frightened in the days before his disappearance
- Location discovered: Aldous Krenn's merchant shop, Commerce District
- NPC introduced: Aldous Krenn (missing merchant, not yet found)

## Session 6 — The Acolyte
- Party located the compromised Temple acolyte identified in Session 4
- Confrontation: acolyte revealed he'd been coerced by a Crown operative known only as "the Architect"
- The Architect appears to be coordinating Crown operations in at least three city districts
- Acolyte's current status: in hiding, party has promised to protect him if he testifies
- NPC introduced: Brother Edwyn (compromised acolyte)
- Plot thread advanced: Temple infiltration — from suspected to confirmed

## Session 7 — Flagon Night (memory reconstruction)
- Roleplay-focused evening at the Silver Flagon; no combat
- Mira reiterated concern about missing merchant Aldous Krenn; party committed to continue investigation
- Morwen disclosed to the party that her sister works for the Serpent Crown
- Aldous Krenn remains missing; party continues the disappearance thread
