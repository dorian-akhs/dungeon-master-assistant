# Session 001: The Silver Roads Begin

> **Quick Summary**: The party arrived in Tolvern and established themselves at the Silver Flagon. Mira sized them up and gave them lodging. The city made its first impressions — prosperous, busy, and just slightly wrong if you look at the edges.

## Events

### Arrival — South Gate

Party entered Tolvern through the South Gate after an uneventful journey. Customs inspection was cursory but the guard seemed to recognize their faces from somewhere — couldn't place it.

### Silver Flagon — First Night

Mira Harrow offered them lodging in a back room at a reasonable rate. Her first words: "You look like trouble. Trouble is at least interesting." Party accepted. Over drinks, she gave them a brief and pragmatic orientation to the Tanner's District.
