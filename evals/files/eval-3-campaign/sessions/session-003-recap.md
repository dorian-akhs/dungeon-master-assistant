# Session 003: The Fence's Silence

> **Quick Summary**: The party used Mira's contact Dross to evaluate the cargo manifests. He confirmed organized Crown activity but refused to name names. On the way back, they were ambushed — light injuries, no fatalities.

Dross (fence, Mira contact) reviewed the manifests. Confirmed: at least four merchant firms in Tolvern are acting as Crown fronts. Refused to name them. "Not worth it to me" was the only explanation offered.

### Street Ambush — Tanner's District

Two assailants, lightly armed, professional. Morwen and Cassiel sustained minor wounds. The attackers fled when Dax's illusions complicated the situation. No capture; no identification.
