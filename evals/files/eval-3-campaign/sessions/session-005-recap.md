# Session 005: The Empty Stool

> **Quick Summary**: Mira mentioned a missing regular — Aldous Krenn. The party investigated and found evidence of a frightened man who had vanished, possibly under Crown pressure.

## Events

### Silver Flagon — Mira's Concern

Over morning tea, Mira mentioned Aldous Krenn hadn't been in for over a week. He'd been a fixture for years. "He didn't say goodbye. Aldous always says goodbye."

### Commerce District — Krenn's Shop

Party found the shop locked. Last entry in the external ledger (for delivery receipts) was eight days ago. A neighbor reported that Aldous had seemed frightened in his final days — looking over his shoulder, turning away customers.

No signs of struggle in or around the shop. He either left voluntarily or was removed carefully.
