# Session 006: The Acolyte Breaks

> **Quick Summary**: The party found and confronted the compromised Temple acolyte — Brother Edwyn. He broke under gentle pressure and revealed a figure called "the Architect" coordinating Crown operations across multiple districts. He's now in hiding under the party's protection.

## Events

### Temple of the All-Witness — Locating Edwyn

Cassiel's prior investigation paid off. Party identified the acolyte (Brother Edwyn) through careful observation — he showed the behavioral markers Cassiel had flagged. Brief, careful approach during his afternoon break.

### Temple Garden — Confrontation

Cassiel approached Edwyn alone, quietly, with no threat implied. Edwyn lasted about three minutes before the weight of it came out — he'd been passing information about Temple security rotations and visiting dignitaries to a Crown operative.

He named his handler only as "the Architect" — a figure who apparently coordinates Crown operations across the Tanner's, Temple, and Commerce districts. Edwyn has never met him/her directly; communication was through dead drops.

Edwyn is terrified. He cooperated fully once the initial conversation opened up.

### Party Decision

Party agreed to protect Edwyn in exchange for his full cooperation. He's currently hidden at a location the DM knows but the transcript doesn't specify.
