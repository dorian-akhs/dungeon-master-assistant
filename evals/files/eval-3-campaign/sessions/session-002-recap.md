# Session 002: Clean Shoes

> **Quick Summary**: Mira warned the party they were being watched. They tracked the observer to a Dockside warehouse and found their first solid evidence of Serpent Crown activity — falsified cargo manifests.

## Events

### Silver Flagon — Mira's Warning

Mira mentioned that someone had been asking around about the party: "Guild-adjacent looking, very clean shoes." Party took this seriously and began surveillance work.

### Dockside District — Warehouse Surveillance

Morwen tracked the observer to a warehouse in the Dockside District. Multi-hour stakeout; eventually got eyes inside. Evidence of cargo being re-labeled with different origin stamps.

### Warehouse Interior

Brief infiltration. Party secured copies of three cargo manifests showing the same goods under different merchant names across multiple trips. Strong evidence of organized fraud — potentially Serpent Crown.
